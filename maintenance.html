How to use this Learning Station

On this Learning Station, you can read Bettercare books and test your knowledge.

  1. Choose a book by tapping on its cover. That will open its contents page.
  2. Choose a chapter from the contents page.
  3. Read the chapter, or tap 'Open chapter test' to test your knowledge.

Order books for training and study

Training courses and studying are more effective with high-quality printed books. To order books quickly and easily, contact Bettercare.

Continuing Professional Development

If your institution subscribes to the Bettercare CPD system, you can earn points for completing chapter tests. To sign up, contact Bettercare.
